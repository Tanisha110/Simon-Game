Press A Key To Start

Rules >

1-At the start of the game, when you press any key on your keyboard , one of the 4 keys click at random, simultaneously emitting the sound associated with that key.

2- It's then the player's turn to press the same key. the catch is to remeber the sequence and click the correct pattern each time.

3- As the level rises one color gets added to the pattern, if you chose wrong button at any point of time, the game ends and you might restart by pressing one of the keys on your keyboard again.
